Your session ID lets you restore your DM notes on another device. Save it somewhere safe.
HOW PLAYERS CONNECT:
Players enter a character name in their sheet and hit ☁ Sync. They get a Character ID — they give that to you, and you paste it into the Load Character box. Their sheet then updates live.
